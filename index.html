ABOUT RESTAURANT

"100 ROKIV TOMU VPERED" IS A FUNDAMENTALLY NEW FORM OF UKRAINIAN CUISINE WITH ITS OWN SUPERFOODS, AMAZING RECIPES AND RECONSIDERED TRADITIONS.
Opened on March 21, 2019 by Inna Popereshniuk and Yevhen Klopotenko, who is the chef of the establishment. The restaurant is located in Kyiv in a historic building on Volodymyrska Street, not far from the Church of the Tithe and Andriyivsky Uzviz.
In the fall of 2020, "NV" magazine named one of the 100 best restaurants in Ukraine. In 2020 and 2021, the establishment became a finalist of the "Salt" restaurant award among restaurants of Ukrainian cuisine.
The design of the restaurant reflects its main goal: to nurture love for traditional Ukrainian cuisine with the help of a modern and creative approach.

MENU

Thus, we are now developing two formats.
